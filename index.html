About us

Menofia Racing Team

Menofia Racing Team is a passionate group of engineering students from Egypt, united by our shared love for motorsports and innovation. Established with the goal of pushing the boundaries of automotive technology, we participate in both national and international competitions, showcasing our technical skills, creativity, and dedication.

Our team is composed of talented individuals specializing in various fields, including mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, aerodynamics, and software development. Together, we work tirelessly to innovate, learn, and improve, aiming not just to compete, but to lead in the development of sustainable transportation solutions. At MRT, we believe in the power of collaboration and learning by doing. Our journey is driven by the ambition to make a mark in the world of motorsports while contributing to the global shift towards greener, more efficient technologies. Join us as we race towards the future, one challenge at a time!.
